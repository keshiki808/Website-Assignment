Twin Peaks is a murder mystery television series that first premiered on April 8th, 1990 on ABC. It was written and created by American novelist Mark Frost and critically acclaimed movie director David Lynch. It was a huge departure from television series at the time, viewers were captivated, tuning in every week to see FBI agent Dale Cooper try to find the killer of a popular high school girl in a rural American community. It garnered a cult following after the series ended and in contemporary times is considered one of the greatest TV shows of all time by reviewers.
